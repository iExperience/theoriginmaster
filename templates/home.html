Week 1

You arrive in Cape Town

Class begins, you explore the city, and you make sixteen new friends. It is the beginning of an epic summer (winter).

With the pre-work under your belt (or maybe not quite finished), a huge amount of work was thrown right at your face on day 1. Maybe you hated Rafi and Allie - replicating websites is tough. Let's hope that you never, ever have to look at Delicio again. By day two and three, your prospects looked a bit better. HTML is just boxes inside boxes? There are multiple ways to align divs on a line? No way.

By the end of week 1, everyone had a personal website on the real internet. You probably struggled a bit, still wish that one div has a bit less padding, and want to perfect your website. But think about it - just one week before your website had a huge picture, little styling, and a welcoming "Hello World". In just 5 days, you came a long way. And hopefully you ended up liking Allie and Rafi after all.
